# Salary Analysis Dashboard – Power BI

## Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing employee compensation data across job titles and years, covering headcount, base pay, overtime, and total pay benefits for public-sector employees.

## Dataset
- 148.7K employees
- Years covered: 2011–2014
- Fields: Job title, base pay, overtime pay, other pay, total pay & benefits

## Key Findings
- Total pay across the workforce reached **11.11bn**, with an average total pay & benefit of **93.69K** per employee.
- Overtime pay accounted for **6.8%** of total pay, while other pay made up **4.9%**.
- Registered Nurses and Transit Operators had the highest base pay by job title, at **0.50bn** and **0.48bn** respectively.
- Transit Operators earned the most in overtime pay overall (**105M**), followed by Firefighters (**86M**).
- The highest individual overtime earner was a Deputy Sheriff, earning **366,349** in overtime pay alone.
- Transit Operators, Registered Nurses, Firefighters, Police Officers, and Deputy Sheriffs made up the largest shares of total pay by job title.

## Dashboard Features
- Year filter (2011–2014) for time-based analysis
- Job title breakdown across base pay, overtime, and total pay
- Top 10 highest-earning employees by overtime pay and total pay & benefits
- Visual breakdown of total pay distribution by job title (pie chart)
